Given a string, compute recursively (no loops) a new string where all the lowercase 'x' chars have been changed to 'y' chars.


changeXY("codex") → "codey"
changeXY("xxhixx") → "yyhiyy"
changeXY("xhixhix") → "yhiyhiy"
